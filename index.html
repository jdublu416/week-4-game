CrystalsCollector!

Instructions:

GameBoard:

  1. Click start to begin!
  2. Each of the four crystal pictures below will have a different value assigned randomly. By clicking on the crystal, you will add points to your total score.
  3. The object of the game is to click the crystals as many times as it takes to match the number to your total score. If you go over...you lose!
  4. The value of the crystal will be revealed to you when you click on it, keeping that same value throughout the game. It is important to remember how much your total score changed!
  5. Each time a new game is started, the Crystals change their value!
    When game over, reset the scores and then push start when you're ready to begin!
